About Us

At Wine Down, we believe that every sip of wine should be an experience to remember. Our passion for wine is what sets us apart from the rest. We carefully select each of our wines to ensure that they are of the highest quality and provide a unique taste experience that will leave you wanting more. Whether you're a seasoned wine enthusiast or just starting out, we welcome you to join us and discover the wonderful world of wine.

Wine Tasting

Come join us for a wine tasting experience like no other. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you through a selection of our finest wines, providing insight into their unique characteristics and origins. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned wine enthusiast, our wine tastings are sure to leave you with a greater appreciation for the art of winemaking.

Winery Orchard

Our winery orchard is where the magic happens. With acres of carefully tended vines, we produce some of the finest grapes in the region. Our winemakers use these grapes to create the unique blends that make up our signature wines. Come take a stroll through the orchard and experience the beauty and serenity of our vineyard.

Fruits

In addition to our grapes, we also grow a variety of other fruits in our orchard. From crisp apples to juicy peaches, our fruit selection is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Take a basket and pick your own, or try some of our delicious fruit jams and preserves.

Wine Bottles

Our wine bottles are a work of art, each one carefully crafted to reflect the unique qualities of the wine inside. From the smooth curves of our red wine bottles to the elegant simplicity of our white wine bottles, every detail is designed to enhance your wine-drinking experience. And with a wide selection of varietals and vintages, there's something for everyone at Wine Down.
